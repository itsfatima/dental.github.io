Overview

IT COMM PVT LTD (ITC) a subsidiary of Punjab Group of Colleges (PGC) was founded as consultants and end-to-end solution providers in the ICT sector. Over a short period of time, we have rapidly evolved into a trusted business technology advisor and strategic partner with many reputable local and international organizations. Our solutions span the entire spectrum of ICT, IoT, Smart cities, and Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)

ITC specializes in transforming ideas into opportunities by using Transfer of Technology (ToT) methods including but not limited to Licensing, Support Contracts, Joint Ventures, Franchising, Strategic Alliances, Turnkey Agreements, Equipment Acquisition and Project Management Services.

Vision
